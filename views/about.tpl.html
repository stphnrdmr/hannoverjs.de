What it is all about

As There're many JavaScript usergroups out there, like Cologne.js, Berlin.js, Munich.js and Hamburg.js, Christoph Burgdorf decided to bring another JavaScript usergroup to life in Hannover. So here it is!

Hannover.js is a usergroup focused on JavaScript and related stuff. The idea has gained great interest around lower saxonys JavaScript folks. We meet regularly on the 4th thursday each month at 7pm. at the NewStore Hannover office, Bödekerstraße 56, 30161 Hannover.

Our sessions focus on specific subjects, such as using JavaScript Patterns, or developing with frameworks. Other meetups are more open and collaborative, e.g. show n’ tells and hack nights. We put as much emphasis into learning new things, as into coming together as a community and making connections that are useful in our daily work as web developers.

You can keep up to date by following Hannover.js' tweets, sending us a mail, or visting our Facebook Page.
